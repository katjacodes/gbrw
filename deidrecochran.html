Deidre Cochran

(she/her)

AJ

I'm excited to take on the role of AJ because she's so multi-faceted. She's strong, determined, messy, goofy and kind all at once. It's not every day you get to portray female characters like that. Having the opportunity to help tell a story you don't often see is an amazing gift and I can't wait to share it!

Deidre has been a Minneapolis-based actor since 2016. Recent works include Miss Shingle in A Gentleman’s Guide to Love and Murder with Old Log Theatre, Marian Anderson in First Lady Suite with Theatre Elision and Mrs. Corey/Ensemble in Mary Poppins with Artistry.
