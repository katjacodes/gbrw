Antonia Perez

(she/her)

Choreographer

I’m excited to explore and show a world that has not been represented nearly enough on stage.

Antonia is an actress and choreographer in the Twin Cities. Her last gig as a choreographer was for Jefferson Township Sparkling Junior Talent Pageant at Park Square Theatre.

She has worked as an actress with many theaters including Theatre Latte Da (Once: The Musical), Park Square Theatre (Antigone), Trademark Theatre (The Hollow), Umbrella Collective (Velvet Swing), Artistry (Legally Blonde), and Theatre Coup d’Etat (Moby Dick).
