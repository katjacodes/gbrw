Gracie Anderson

(she/they)

Katie

I’m so honored to play the role of Katie and tell the story of a queer woman’s journey to self-discovery and love. I’m also more than thrilled to play best friends on stage with my real life best friend, Deidre Cochran.

Previous Credits: Park Square Theatre (The Rocky Horror Show- Dr. Frank-N-Furter), Old Log Theater (Tenderly- Rosemary Clooney, Beehive- Janis Joplin), Lyric Arts Main Street Stage (Always… Patsy Cline- Patsy Cline), Artistry (Hairspray- Tracy Turnblad, The Secret Garden- Martha, She Loves Me- Ilona Ritter), Renegade Theater Company (The Who’s Tommy- The Acid Queen, Lippa’s The Wild Party- Kate), 7th House Theater (Hair, Little Shop of Horrors, Jonah and the Whale), Yellow Tree Theater (A Gone Fishin’ Christmas- Mischa) and many more.

Gracie can also be heard belting her face off on the podcast Take It With You, Live Radio Theater from Duluth, which is available wherever you listen to podcasts and at takeitwithyou.org. In 2016, Gracie was named Best Emerging Actress in Minneapolis City Pages. Gracie is a graduate of the Perpich Center for Arts Education and a holder of a B.F.A. in Musical Theater from the University of Minnesota, Duluth.
