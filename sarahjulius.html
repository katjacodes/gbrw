Sarah Julius

(she/they)

Playwright, Composer, Lyricist, Executive Producer

The commitment and talent of this team inspires me, humbles me, and makes me want to work even harder to bring this musical to life. I’m so excited to share this Great Big Rainbow World with each and every one of you.

Sarah is the creator of Great Big Rainbow World and she’s beyond excited to have found such talented theater industry pros to collaborate with on this project that’s so near and dear to her heart.

Sarah is originally from Iowa City and moved to the Twin Cities in July 2004 to play rugby and get a big kid job. She’s overjoyed her path has found her here on the precipice of her biggest dream - to write and produce a musical - and is so excited to share her story with the world in June 2022.
