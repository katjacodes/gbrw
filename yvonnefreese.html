Yvonne Freese

(she/they)

Erin

I am delighted to join GBRW as Berit. I was a two sport athlete in college and am just thrilled to be a part of bringing more athletic womxn to the stage! I have always admired the tough, raw nature of rugby and can't wait to explore the game both on and off the stage.

Yvonne is thrilled to be joining GBRW and to work with a team of such strong, inspiring womxn. Freese continues to work as an actor, singer and puppeteer in the Twin Cities having spent the past year performing with Mystery Science Theater 3000. Representative theater; performing on “A Prairie Home Companion,” Stages- Mom (Fancy Nancy), Teatro del Pueblo-Various (Coco's Barrio), Buns and GunsLLC- Linda (Spandex the Musical), Open Eye- puppeteer (Juan Bobo), puppeteer (2-wheel tour), MMT- Sarah (High Fidelity) Felice (Love Comics), Really Spicy Opera- Mother (Amahl), Gilda cover (Rigoletto), STG-Cinderella’s mother/Giant/Granny (Into the Woods).
