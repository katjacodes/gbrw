Kyia Britts

(she/her)

Lighting Designer

I’m very excited to be welcomed into the Great Big Rainbow World family and can’t wait to get started on this project!

Kyia is a freelance lighting designer based in the Twin Cities, Lighting Director for Totino-Grace High School, and is the Marketing, Programming & Development Coordinator for Children’s Performing Arts. She received Bachelor of Science degrees in Marketing Communications and Theatre Arts from the University of Wisconsin - River Falls.

She has designed for Lakeshore Players Theatre, Children’s Performing Arts, Totino-Grace High School, Merrill Community Arts Center, UW-River Falls Theater, and Park Square Theater.
