Kendall Kent

(she/her)

Ensemble

What most excites me about working on GBRW is the people involved. In most rehearsal rooms, it's nearly impossible to have a team completely made up of women and gender non-conforming individuals, but this is exactly the team needed to tell these stories in the most truthful way possible.

Kendall is a graduate of the University of Minnesota/Guthrie Theatre BFA Actor Training Program and has worked all around the Twin Cities. You can usually find her producing and performing Shakespeare in the back room of bars with BARd Shakes. Most recently: Twelfth Night, The Comedy of Errors, As You Like It, and Much Ado About Nothing. Other credits include: Amadeus at Gremlin Theatre, Hooded at Mixed Blood Theatre, Jack and the Beanstalk and Charlotte's Web with Old Log Theatre, Sex, War, and Syphilis with Theatre Unbound, and more.
